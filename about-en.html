About Outlander Location Tours

We run small-group and private Outlander-themed tours across Scotland – crafted for fans of the series and for anyone eager to discover real Scottish history and landscapes.

What we provide

  • Small-group and private tours to filming locations and historic sites.
  • Guides who connect scenes from the series with real events and places.
  • Custom itineraries for schools, colleges, and history groups.
  • Comfortable coaches, including accessible options upon request.
